Clarence’s Coding Journey

Building, Learning, and Growing in the World of Code

Hi, I'm Clarence!

I’m 13 years old, and I’ve been coding for a while now. I love creating things with code, whether it’s websites, apps, or simple games. I'm always learning new skills, and this website is where I share my journey.

Why This Website?

I built this website to track my progress as I learn software development. It’s a place where I can share my projects, learnings, and challenges with the world. I hope to inspire other young developers and show them what’s possible with coding!

My Family Inspiration

My family has always been a huge support system. My parents encourage me to follow my passion for coding, and my siblings are always excited to see the projects I work on. Their belief in me keeps me motivated to push through challenges and keep learning.

What I’ve Learned So Far

  • HTML: The foundation of web development. Learned how to structure web pages.
  • CSS: Styling websites with colors, fonts, and layouts.
  • JavaScript: Making websites interactive with dynamic features.
  • Python: Exploring programming beyond the web and solving problems.
  • Web Development: Starting to learn how to create complete websites with both front-end and back-end technologies.

Goals for the Future

I want to become a **full-stack developer**—someone who can build everything from the front-end to the back-end of websites and apps. I also want to learn more about **game development** and start building my own games. In the future, I’d love to explore **machine learning** and even contribute to open-source projects!
